Not sure how to edit an existing event?

1. Find an existing event.

2. Click on the existing event. A pop-up will pop up, as shown below.

3. Click on "more details >>".

4. When you are redirected to your personal calendar, click on the desired event.

5. Locate the three dots that form the "Options" button, and click on them, as shown below.

6. Once you click on the "Options" button, a dropdown list will appear. Click on "Duplicate".

7. Have fun editing your event!


Need help on deleting an existing event?

1. Find the existing event.

2. Click on the existing event. A pop-up will pop up, as shown below.

3. Click on "more details>>".

4. When you are redirected to your personal calendar, click on the desired event.

5. Locate the trash bin, and click on it, as shown below.

6. Your event has been deleted!
