Time to have some fun!


It's always important to take care of yourself first. Before you explore the activities, always remember:


  • You ARE enough!!!
  • It's okay to say "no" to people.
  • Actions speak louder than words.
  • Putting yourself first is not selfish.
  • All bad things will come to an end.
  • Take a deep breath when feeling nervous or anxious.
  • Forgiving yourself and others is crucial to moving on in life.
  • There may always be people who judge you, so do what makes you happy.
  • Making a mistake will not be the end of the world; you can always learn from your mistakes.
  • You can't control how situations will work out or how people feel about you. You can only control how you react.



Below are some outdoor activities to escape the indoors and get some fresh air!


  1. Read a book in the park
  2. Go on a walk or bike ride
  3. Plant some plants such as flowers
  4. Express your feelings in a notebook
  5. Watch the sunrise or sunset at the beach.
  6. Make a charcuterie board and have a picnic
  7. Draw or paint a masterpiece (or something simple!)
  8. Travel to a new location and explore all the places there
  9. Go to the gym, just exercising in general can boost your mood
  10. Browse through a bookstore with a friend and pick books for each other



Below are some indoor activities if you don't feel like socializing!


  1. Take a relaxing bath
  2. Express your feelings in a notebook
  3. Cook your favorite meal or try a new recipe
  4. Draw or paint a masterpiece (or something simple!)
  5. Watch the show or movie you have been waiting to watch
  6. Write to your future self of the goals you wish to accomplish
  7. Have a dance party by yourself or a virtual on with your friends!
  8. Cuddle your pet, after all they do say dogs are a man's best friend
  9. Rearrange your living space. It can be as small as your desk or big as your room.
  10. Have a (virtual) fashion show! Put on your best clothes and walk the runway in your best catwalk
